Interconnect

Find connections and learn anything

December 12, 2024

Interconnect was made in collaboration with Julia Yu, Cassidy Katona, and Jackson Mohler, over the span of around 2 months. The app aims at helping people who want to get into a new hobby or skill by connecting them with volunteer or mentor who is already knowledgeable about the given topic.

Features:

  • Matching mechanics between a mentee and a mentor
  • Communication between a mentee and a mentor (one-on-one chat)
  • Profile customization
