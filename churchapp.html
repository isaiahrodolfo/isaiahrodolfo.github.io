Church App

Access livestreams, take notes on lectures, and daily readings

January 28, 2025

This project is currently in development.

I am leading this project until production and maintenance. I am currently in the process of gathering requirements, designing the database system, and app layout.

Some features of this app will include:

  • Taking notes live, reflecting the current slide of the presentation
  • Keep track of your progress on courses, and view next dates of instruction
  • Daily reading and reflection, and share your findings and progress with others
  • Sign up for events and receive notifications
  • Admin features: create events, assign tasks, randomly assign small groups
This app is anticipated to be used by over 100 users weekly. This is my biggest project so far.
